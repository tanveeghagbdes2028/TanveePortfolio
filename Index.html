3. From Seed To Spoon

  • Designed a 6×6-inch children’s book for ages 3–6, showing the journey of vegetables from seed to spoon.
  • Teaches children about the hard work of farmers and the importance of eating healthy food.
  • Encourages kids to choose fresh vegetables over junk food by understanding where food comes from.
  • Initial version in Adobe Illustrator lacked engagement, colour use, and visual hierarchy.
  • Redesigned in Adobe Photoshop with bright colours, clear hierarchy, and interactive elements to increase engagement.
  • Final book features simple language and playful illustrations suitable for young children.
  • The story follows a farmer and a curious young girl who explores the farm and learns about each stage of farming.
